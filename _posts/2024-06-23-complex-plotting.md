## The Main Bijection

When looking to plot complex functions, we'll use a fun and simple fact:

$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathbb{R} \times \mathbb{R} &\cong \mathbb{C} \\
(x, y) &\mapsto x+iy
\end{aligned}
$$
